HOW TO PLAY

Some asshat at work left you a bunch of unfinished curcuit boards where they only soldered on the components.

It's now up to you to finish the curcuits, just remember to power all the components and reach the output before the power in the battery runs out! The starting point is in the top right and you have to exit in the bottom left.

Components can take power from all sides aswell as output power on all sides (but do not power adjacent components), but the paths you can move only takes power from one side and outputs to one other side, as represented by the arrows.
